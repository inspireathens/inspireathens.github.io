Before we get into any activities, we may introduce ourselves, get some conversations going. Meanwhile the organizers make sure that the space is prepared with the appropriate setup.


After warming up the environment and ourselves, we begin the schedule that is divided in four stages:


Stage 1, Discussions.

Stage 2, Workshops.

Stage 3, Missions.

Stage 4, Jam-Sessions.

We begin a gathering with discussions in many forms. Talking about something in order to reach a decision or to exchange information, ideas, opinions, etc. A discussion can also help to resolve conflicts and enhance understanding.

chilling

We provide spaces of equipment and materials for the participants to get practically involved. We do our best to enhance our workshop spaces with more resources, bringing more possibilities. Workshops start after the discussions end.
After workshops, we focus on creating products that can later be used in the market. This process helps us to sustain and develop our non-profit organization. Missions also help participants to build a portfolio of works.
